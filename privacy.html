Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in how it is handled.

1. Data Collection

We collect your name, phone number, and verified CNIC details to ensure trust between parties. We store the content of your agreements, including attached proofs (images/documents), on secure cloud servers.

2. Camera Usage

  • Likhat may request access to your device camera to capture images of documents or proof related to agreements.
  • These images are only used within agreements and are securely stored.
  • The app does not access your camera without your permission.

3. Data Security

All user data is encrypted during transmission and while stored in our databases. We implement strict access controls so that only authorized participants in an agreement can view its content. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

Likhat uses Firebase (by Google) for authentication, database management, and secure data storage. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Information Sharing

  • We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • Data may only be shared with government authorities when required by law or to investigate fraud or illegal activity.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Any significant changes to this policy will be notified through the app. Continued use of Likhat means you agree to the latest Privacy Policy.

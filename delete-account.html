Account Deletion & Data Rights

We take data ownership seriously. However, as a platform for mutual agreements, account deletion is subject to the integrity of shared records.

When You CAN Delete

  • You have no active agreements in progress.
  • All your past agreements are completed, cancelled, or closed by all parties.
  • You do not have any pending verification requests.

When You CANNOT Delete

  • You are currently a party to an ongoing agreement.
  • The other party requires the record for mutual proof.
  • You have unresolved financial or service disputes within the platform.
⚠️ Reason: To prevent the loss of shared records and preserve legal integrity for all participating parties.

Data Handling Breakdown

When an account is deleted, we apply the following treatment to your data to maintain platform integrity:

Data Type Action Taken Status
Personal Profile (Name, Phone) Permanently Purged DELETED
Login Credentials Permanently Purged DELETED
Agreement Content Archived for other parties RETAINED
CNIC Metadata Hashed for compliance RETAINED

*Agreement records must be retained to preserve the evidence for the other party involved in the contract. Requests are processed within 30 days.
